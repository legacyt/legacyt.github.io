Quick thoughts

Below is a list of random ideas that have occurred to me that seemed worth writing down. Some of them are worth following up on, some are not.

2019

19Jan.1 Spinning pool

Thought

I’d be interested in seeing how a spinning infinite pool worked. Something like a tilt a whirl. Would probably need to use denser salt water to make sure people don’t get pinned to the bottom and drown – treading water likely isn’t feasible. I don’t know what the acceleration forces on one of those rides is – swimming may not be realistic, though to some extent, a pool that slopes at 45° would probably also be interesting without being so impossible to function in. I suspect there would be some challenging currents until the water stabilized, but simultaneously, it’s not really the kind of pool one could jump into so the currents would have to be tolerable for this to work. I’m imagining a torus with a flat bottom, with a foot or two of water in it. Sit in the water, gently begin rotating the device, and the water and people get pulled to the outside. Maintain the acceleration until the desired water angle is reached. After some experience, a more tailored shape could be developed to optimize the swimming channel at some particular angle.

At a smaller, much more obtainable and safe scale, a spinning aquarium could test the idea.
